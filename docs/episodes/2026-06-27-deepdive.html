Deep Dive: Text diffusion: language models that fill in the blanks instead of going left to right

This week's iLLaDA — an 8B language model trained from scratch with diffusion, not autoregression — is the doorway to how masked diffusion language models actually work: generation by confidence-first iterative unmasking, why it kills the reversal curse and makes infilling native, and why the parallel-speed promise is still blocked by tokens chosen in the same step being unable to coordinate.
