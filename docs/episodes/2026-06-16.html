What the recall was really about, and the week harness beat model

Self-Attention — 2026-06-16

Tuesday, June 16, 2026

The week's news lived around the model, not in it. The Fable/Mythos export-control recall turned out to hinge on the difference between asking a coding model to "review code for security issues" and to "fix this code" — and seventy-six cybersecurity experts signed an open letter calling the ban dangerous. Around it: a 1.5B model (VibeThinker) doing competition math for $7,800, Microsoft's FastContext explorer cutting coding-agent tokens up to 60%, a16z-backed reliability startup Probably betting on the harness over the model, and Artificial Analysis re-weighting its Intelligence Index toward agents. Closing on SpaceX's $60B Cursor deal against a layoff "powder keg."
