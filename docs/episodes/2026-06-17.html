The day's through-line is control of model access. Z.ai's open-weights GLM-5.2 became the first open model past 80% on Terminal-Bench and matched the closed frontier on long-horizon coding at roughly one-sixth the cost — while new reporting says the U.S. order that took Anthropic's Fable 5 and Mythos 5 offline was political, not a real jailbreak, and security researchers are protesting that it harms U.S. cyber defense. Around it: Nvidia's open hybrid Mamba-transformer Nemotron 3 Ultra, OpenAI's Deployment Simulation evaluation method, Alibaba's Qwen-Robot embodied suite, and SpaceX's reported \$60B all-stock acquisition of Cursor.
