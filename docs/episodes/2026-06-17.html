Open weights crash the leaderboard, and the kill switch comes up at the G7

Self-Attention — 2026-06-17

Wednesday, June 17, 2026

Open-weight models had a leaderboard-rearranging day: Z.ai's GLM-5.2 became the top open-weights model on Artificial Analysis's newly agent-weighted Intelligence Index — and effectively level with GPT-5.5 on agentic work at a fraction of the cost — while DeepSeek closed a ~$7.4B first external round at a $50B+ valuation. At the G7, Macron and Modi warned the U.S. could "turn off the switch" on American AI, a fear last week's Anthropic Fable/Mythos export-control blackout made concrete. Plus OpenAI's near-autonomous AI chemist and LifeSciBench, fresh public-trust data (Pew: 16% positive; 60% say "AI" branding is a turnoff), and a big day for world models (Odyssey's $1.45B round, Qwen-RobotWorld, MolmoMotion).
