Trained to be honest, too guarded to grade, and Norway pulls the plug

Self-Attention — 2026-06-20

Saturday, June 20, 2026

A measurement-and-trust day. OpenAI's alignment team reports that reinforcement learning directly on beneficial traits (honesty, transparency, epistemic humility) generalizes broadly and shows "selective persistence" — easy to steer toward good behavior, harder to push toward deception. Meanwhile Anthropic's Fable 5 has become hard for independent evaluators to grade because its hidden safeguards silently route flagged prompts to a weaker model; Nvidia released Nemotron 3 Ultra, a 550B open-weight hybrid Mamba-Attention MoE with open training data; the Model Context Protocol shipped stable enterprise-managed authorization (Okta first); and Norway announced a near-total ban on generative AI in elementary schools.
